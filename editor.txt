left click - place object/tile
right click - remove object/tile

Hotkeys:
[ - decrease cursor Z
] - incease cursor Z

Click Modifiers:
E - while holding `E` key, right click remove empty tiles (like grass), while left click places on top of empty tiles.  Without `E`, objects get placed inside of grass
N - place sprite rotated
M - place sprite mirrored
R - pick random object from selected sprite bank. Useful for placing decorations, like bushes
